This is a custom color palette that I chose by adjusting the circle dials in the color wheel. I use a soft tone of grey, baby blue, silver, dark grey, sand black. I discovered that these colors compliment themselves well along with the light text that allows easy reading. Based on color psychology, these colors represent areas of happiness, sincerity, and purity which are all important aspects to music!
